What's On

ACTS
- A mix of original/new and classic/familiar, Rock, Swing Era Retro, Electronica, New talent - Youngsters!
Folk Songwriters, Acoustic Harmony, Agit Pop.
PLUS
- A one off unique abridged, specially for Silverbury, version of the
stage musical 'The Man Who Sewed The World', about Freddie Burretti, David Bowie's clothes designer taylor and stylist,
featuring an original script, choreography, David Bowie songs and others sounds from the era.
ENDING
- In a massive celebration of dance floor fillers through the decades ... Motown funk soul and disco from Groovestars

Where

The Firs, 69 Little London, Silverstone, NN12 8UP

Parking and entry through New Rookery Farm.

When

Saturday the 8th of June

Doors open at 12 noon.

What

A day of music, food and fun, organised by volunteers from the community.

Why

All proceeds are raised for local and international charities.
